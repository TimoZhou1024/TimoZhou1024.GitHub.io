I am an undergraduate student in Data Science and Big Data Technology at Fudan University. My research interests include structured representation learning, generative modeling, optimal transport, model editing, and trustworthy and explainable AI.

I focus on building efficient, robust, and interpretable learning systems for complex, incomplete, and heterogeneous data. My recent work includes optimal-transport-guided flow matching for incomplete and unaligned multi-view clustering, and exact null-space model editing for medical Vision Transformers.

I am planning to apply to PhD programs in the United States for Fall 2027. I welcome research discussions, collaboration opportunities, and conversations about potential PhD opportunities.

## Research Interests

- Structured Representation Learning
- Generative Modeling and Flow Matching
- Optimal Transport for Machine Learning
- Model Editing and Explainable AI
- Trustworthy AI for Scientific and Medical Applications
